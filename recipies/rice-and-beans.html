Rice and Beans

Description

A SUPER simple and easy spanish style rice and bean that's easy to make when you need a fix.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups water
  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 1 package sazon seasoning
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons adobo seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 can of beans (red or black preferred)
  • 1 can pitted olives

Steps

  1. Boil rice: Bring the rice to a boil
  2. Mix in seasonings: Mix in sazon, adobo, and garlic. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook covered for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix in the rest: Uncover and mix in beans and olives. Cook until liquid has been absorbed (~10 minutes) and mix before serving.
