Cheesy Pasta

Description

An easy throw together pasta that you can make quickly for a fast lunch/dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 serving uncooked pasta of choice
  • 1 cup shredded cheese of choice
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp Butter

Steps

  1. Boil noodles: Bring the noodles to a boil and cook until tender. Drain when done.
  2. Cut veggies: Cut the zucchini and pepper into acceptable shapes. Season with italian seasoning but save the excess. Mince the garlic if needed.
  3. Cook veggies: In a small sauce pan add the butter and cook down the veggies until slightly transcluscent.
  4. Add sauce: Add the sauce to the pan along with the cheese and the leftover seasoning from earlier. Let cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add noodles: Add the sauce to the noodles and mix well.
